# F1 Driver Alpha Framework & Competitive Intelligence Platform

Performance intelligence platform analyzing 75 years of Formula 1 history, evaluating 861 drivers across 920 constructor seasons and more than 70,000 race, qualifying, lap-time, championship, and operational records using Python, SQL, Power BI, and advanced analytics to identify the drivers of long-term competitive success.

## Business Problem

Formula 1 performance is traditionally evaluated using championships, race wins, and podiums. However, these metrics often fail to separate driver contribution from machinery advantage.

This project was designed to answer a central question:

**How much of a driver's success comes from driving ability versus the competitiveness of the car?**

The platform develops a data-driven framework capable of comparing drivers across eras, regulations, teams, and competitive environments.

## Key Business Questions

* Who are the greatest Formula 1 drivers of all time when machinery advantage is considered?
* Which drivers consistently outperformed their teammates?
* Which drivers extracted the most value from the opportunities available to them?
* How much does car performance influence championship success?
* Which teams produced the most dominant cars in Formula 1 history?
* Which drivers were elite qualifiers but underperformed in races?
* Which drivers demonstrated the highest long-term consistency?
* How does driver performance compare across different eras of Formula 1?
* Which drivers achieved the greatest results relative to team strength?
* What characteristics are most strongly associated with long-term competitive success?
